Features

  • Company Directory So your jobboard clients can profile themselves.
  • Labelling / Tagging of Jobs and Companies Let users find similar jobs and companies.
  • News Area For quick news and letting your users know what’s happening in your niche.
  • Magazine / Blog For interviews, insights and deeper articles.
